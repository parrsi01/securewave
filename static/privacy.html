Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2024

At SecureWave VPN, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our VPN service.

Our Zero-Log Commitment

We do NOT log, track, or store:

  • Your browsing history or activity
  • IP addresses used while connected
  • DNS queries
  • Connection timestamps or duration
  • Bandwidth usage details
  • Traffic data or content

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, communication, and password recovery
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through third-party payment providers (Stripe, PayPal). We do not store full credit card numbers
  • Account Credentials: Your encrypted password (we cannot see your actual password)

1.2 Technical Information (Anonymous)

We collect minimal anonymous technical data to maintain and improve our service:

  • App Version: To ensure compatibility and provide updates
  • Server Load: Aggregate anonymous metrics to optimize server performance
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous diagnostic data if the app crashes (optional, can be disabled)

1.3 What We Do NOT Collect

We maintain a strict zero-log policy. We do NOT collect:

  • IP addresses while you're connected to our VPN
  • Websites you visit or applications you use
  • DNS queries or requests
  • Connection timestamps or session duration
  • Bandwidth or traffic data
  • Your original IP address or VPN server IP correlation

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Provide the Service: Enable your VPN connection and account management
  • Process Payments: Handle subscription billing through our payment processors
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and technical issues
  • Service Improvement: Analyze anonymous aggregate data to optimize performance
  • Security: Prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • Communications: Send service updates, security alerts, and subscription notifications

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.2 Limited Sharing

We may share information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe and PayPal process payments securely on our behalf
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate our infrastructure (e.g., hosting providers) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request (we will notify you unless prohibited)
  • Business Transfers: If we are acquired or merged, your information may be transferred (you will be notified)

3.3 Transparency Report

We are committed to transparency. We publish an annual transparency report detailing:

  • Number of data requests received (if any)
  • How we responded to those requests
  • Data we were able or unable to provide

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with 24/7 monitoring
  • Access Controls: Strict internal access policies and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Password Protection: Your password is hashed using industry-standard bcrypt

5. VPN Connection Security

Your VPN connection is protected by:

  • SecureWave Core: Modern, secure, and fast connection technology
  • ChaCha20 Encryption: Military-grade symmetric encryption
  • Curve25519 Key Exchange: Secure key exchange for perfect forward secrecy
  • DNS Leak Protection: All DNS queries routed through an encrypted connection
  • Kill Switch: Automatic internet cutoff if VPN connection drops

6. Data Retention

6.1 Account Data

We retain your account information (email, subscription status) as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where required by law for financial records.

6.2 Connection Logs

We do not create connection logs, so there is nothing to retain or delete.

6.3 Payment Records

Payment transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes, but these do not include VPN usage data.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@securewave.app.

8. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal basis for processing: Legitimate interest and contractual necessity
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • Data transfers outside the EEA are protected by appropriate safeguards

9. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately.

11. Cookies and Tracking

11.1 Cookies We Use

We use minimal cookies for essential functionality:

  • Session Cookies: Keep you logged in while using the service
  • Security Cookies: Prevent fraud and protect your account

11.2 No Tracking

We do NOT use:

  • Third-party tracking or analytics cookies
  • Advertising cookies or pixels
  • Cross-site tracking
  • Social media tracking

12. Third-Party Services

12.1 Payment Processors

We use Stripe and PayPal for payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Stripe Privacy Policy
  • PayPal Privacy Policy

12.2 Infrastructure Providers

Our servers are hosted on Microsoft Azure. They process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements.

13. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in multiple countries to provide optimal performance. When you use our Service, your data may be transferred across borders. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting a notice on our website
  • Sending an email to your registered email address
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

15. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Privacy Email: privacy@securewave.app
  • General Support: support@securewave.app
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@securewave.app
  • Contact Form: https://securewave-app.azurewebsites.net/contact.html
